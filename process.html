The Three‑Step Workflow

1

Early Ideation

Goal

Outputs

A spread of promising concepts, ready to curate

Why this matters

Compare multiple approaches in minutes before investing in details

2

Refinement & Systems

Figma Design

This is where the grounded UX work truly happens — mapping journeys, testing logic, and crafting clarity that aligns with user behaviors and business context.

Goal

Outputs

Why this matters

Accelerate sign‑off and reduce translation errors during build
